## рџЊЊ The World

Traverse through 7 distinct biomes, each with its own atmosphere, unique enemies, and secrets:
1. **Ashfall Town** - The last sanctuary of humanity.
2. **Haunted Graveyard** - Where the dead refuse to rest.
3. **Cursed Forest** - A twisting labyrinth of shadows.
4. **Dry Desert** - Endless dunes guarded by armored sentinels.
5. **Palm Coast** - A beautiful beach with dangerous tides.
6. **Frost Fields** - A frozen wasteland cursed by the Titans.
7. **Ancient Ruins & Titan's Arena** - The final climb to the throne.

## вљ”пёЏ Key Features

- **Soulslike Combat**: Precise dodge rolls, parries, and heavy/light attack combos.
- **Mana & Skill System**: Unlock powerful abilities like *Soul Slash*, *Flame Burst*, and the devastating *Void Storm*.
- **Bonfire Progression**: Burn your collected souls to upgrade Vigor, Endurance, Strength, and Attunement.
- **Dynamic Day/Night Cycle**: The world changes as time passes. Lamps light up at dusk, and enemies grow bolder at night.
- **Multi-language Support**: Fully localized in **English** and **Bahasa Indonesia**.
- **Visual Novel Dialogs**: Deep lore and NPC interactions with a cinematic presentation.
- **Optimized for Web**: Features advanced technical optimizations like **Distance Culling**, **Shadow Follow Tracking**, and **Physics Simplification** to ensure a smooth 60 FPS experience.
